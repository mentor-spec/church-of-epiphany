About Us

The Anglican Church of Epiphany is a welcoming community dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Christ through worship, outreach, and service. We are committed to nurturing faith and fostering fellowship among our members and neighbors.

  • Weekly worship services and Bible studies
  • Community outreach programs
  • Youth and children ministries
  • Marriage counseling and family support
